# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is HustleBooks?
HustleBooks is a smart accounting platform designed for small businesses, 
freelancers, and side-hustlers who need simple but powerful bookkeeping without 
the complexity of traditional accounting software.

### When will HustleBooks be available?
We're targeting a public launch in Q4 2026. Stay tuned for updates!

### Is HustleBooks free?
HustleBooks will have a tiered pricing model:
- **Free Tier:** Basic features for individuals
- **Pro Tier:** Advanced features for growing businesses
- **Enterprise:** Custom solutions for larger organizations

### Is my data secure?
Yes! Security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption, 
secure authentication, and follow best practices for data protection.

## Technical Questions

### What platforms will HustleBooks support?
Initially, HustleBooks will be available as:
- Web application (all browsers)
- iOS app (planned)
- Android app (planned)

### Will there be bank integration?
Yes! Bank integration is planned for a future release after the MVP.

### Can I import data from other accounting software?
Import functionality is on our roadmap. Initially, we'll support CSV imports.

## Business Questions

### Is HustleBooks open source?
No, HustleBooks is a proprietary commercial product.

### Do you offer custom enterprise solutions?
Yes! Contact us at sarwar@hustlebooks.io for enterprise inquiries.
